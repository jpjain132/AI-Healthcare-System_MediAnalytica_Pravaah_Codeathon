Our Medical Analysis Tools

Access our specialized tools for medical document analysis, prescription processing, and rare disease detection.

Medical Document Analyzer

Upload and analyze medical documents including lab reports, patient histories, and clinical notes. Our AI extracts key information and identifies patterns to support clinical decision-making.

Text Extraction

Accurately extract text from scanned documents and PDFs with OCR technology.

Data Structuring

Organize extracted information into structured formats for easy review.

Anomaly Detection

Identify abnormal values and potential concerns in medical reports.

Prescription Processor

Process and validate prescriptions to reduce medication errors. Check for drug interactions, appropriate dosages, and patient-specific contraindications.

Drug Interaction Check

Identify potential interactions between prescribed medications.

Dosage Validation

Verify appropriate dosages based on patient age, weight, and condition.

Allergy Detection

Check prescribed medications against known patient allergies.

Rare Disease Detection

Identify potential rare diseases based on symptoms, medical history, and test results. Our system compares patient data against thousands of rare disease profiles.

Symptom Analysis

Analyze symptom patterns to identify potential rare conditions.

Genetic Correlation

Cross-reference genetic markers with known rare disease databases.

Specialist Recommendations

Suggest appropriate specialists based on detected condition probabilities.
